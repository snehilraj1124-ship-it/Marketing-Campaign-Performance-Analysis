# Campaign Dataset Overview

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains information from 25 marketing campaigns conducted across multiple digital marketing channels.

## Marketing Channels

- Facebook
- Instagram
- Google Ads
- YouTube
- LinkedIn
- Email Marketing
- Influencer Marketing

## Metrics Included

- Campaign Budget
- Impressions
- Reach
- Clicks
- CTR
- Conversions
- Conversion Rate
- Revenue
- ROI

## Purpose

The dataset was analyzed to evaluate campaign effectiveness, customer engagement, conversion performance, and return on investment.

## Dataset Scope

Total Campaigns Analyzed: 25

Analysis Period: Multi-Campaign Performance Review

Business Domain: Marketing Analytics
